# Product Management AI Prompt Library

A practical collection of prompts for **Market Research, Competitor Analysis, Pricing Strategy, and Go-to-Market (GTM) Strategy** that product managers can use with GitHub Copilot Chat or any AI assistant.

---

## 1. Market Research Prompt

### Objective

Understand market trends, customer pain points, opportunities, and growth drivers.

### Prompt

```text
Analyze the AI-powered CRM market for mid-sized businesses in North America. Identify the top trends, customer pain points, emerging opportunities, and key growth drivers. Present the findings in a SWOT analysis and summarize the top five insights.
```

### Expected Output

* Market size and growth trends
* Customer pain points
* Emerging opportunities
* Competitive landscape
* SWOT analysis
* Top 5 strategic insights

---

## 2. Competitor Analysis Prompt

### Objective

Compare competing products and identify differentiation opportunities.

### Prompt

```text
Compare our product with Salesforce, HubSpot, and Zoho CRM across pricing, key features, AI capabilities, integrations, target customers, strengths, and weaknesses. Present the comparison in a table and recommend three differentiators we should prioritize.
```

### Expected Output

| Competitor | Pricing         | AI Features | Integrations | Target Segment    | Key Strength   |
| ---------- | --------------- | ----------- | ------------ | ----------------- | -------------- |
| Salesforce | Enterprise      | Advanced AI | Extensive    | Large Enterprises | Ecosystem      |
| HubSpot    | Mid-market      | Moderate AI | Strong       | SMBs              | Ease of use    |
| Zoho CRM   | Budget-friendly | Basic AI    | Moderate     | SMBs              | Cost-effective |

---

## 3. Pricing Strategy Prompt

### Objective

Evaluate pricing models and recommend the best monetization approach.

### Prompt

```text
Recommend a pricing strategy for a SaaS project management tool targeting startups and SMBs. Compare Freemium, Tiered Subscription, and Usage-Based pricing models, explain the pros and cons of each, and suggest the best option with sample pricing tiers.
```

### Expected Output

* Comparison of pricing models
* Pros and cons
* Revenue impact analysis
* Recommended model
* Sample pricing tiers
* Conversion assumptions

---

## 4. Go-to-Market (GTM) Strategy Prompt

### Objective

Create a launch and growth plan for a new product.

### Prompt

```text
Create a go-to-market strategy for launching an AI-powered meeting assistant. Include target audience, value proposition, positioning, pricing, marketing channels, launch timeline, success metrics, risks, and a 90-day execution plan.
```

### Expected Output

* Target audience and personas
* Positioning statement
* Pricing approach
* Marketing and sales channels
* Launch timeline
* Success metrics (KPIs)
* Risk mitigation plan
* 30/60/90-day execution roadmap

---

## Pro Tip: Add Context for Better Results

Before running any prompt, include your company context:

```text
Company: Contoso AI
Product: AI-powered CRM assistant
Target Market: Mid-sized SaaS companies
Current ARR: $2M
Primary Goal: Increase win rate by 15% and expand into the US market
```

This helps the AI generate recommendations that are **more relevant, realistic, and actionable**.

---

## Recommended Workflow

1. **Market Research** → Understand the market.
2. **Competitor Analysis** → Identify gaps and differentiation.
3. **Pricing Strategy** → Define monetization.
4. **GTM Strategy** → Plan the launch and growth motion.

This sequence mirrors a real-world product strategy process and produces outputs that can be directly refined into product briefs, business cases, or executive presentations.
